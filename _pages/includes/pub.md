**Self-Critique Guided Iterative Reasoning for Multi-hop Question Answering**

Zheng Chu, huiming fan, Jingchang Chen, Qianyu Wang, **Mingda Yang**, Jiafeng Liang, Zhongjie Wang, Hao Li, Guo Tang, Ming Liu, Bing Qin
